Freewriting is a simple concept. You practice your ability to write and think more freely, if you force yourself to write nonstop and ignore the little mistakes you - without a doubt - will encounter along the way.

This is great practice for both more effective writing processes and when you want to work in a state of flow.

This is what this tool is for.

Start the excercise now

How does it work?

You write - nonstop - for 10 minutes. Just keep your fingers tapping away. Try to gently remind yourself not to focus on what you've already written. Don't look back. Keep tapping.

So why this program?

This is a very simple writing tool to aid you in practicing freewriting.

  • It automatically counts down from 10 minutes, when you start writing.
  • You can in no way edit your text. Delete and backspace is disabled.
  • If you pause for more than a second the program will start to delete your text. One character at a time.
  • When the time is up your text is locked. And you can copy it, if you need keep it.

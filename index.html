Freewriting

Freewriting is a simple concept. You practice your ability to write and think freely, if you force yourself to not stop and make fixes while you write. It was originally coined by Peter Elbow.

How:You write for 10 minutes. Non-stop. Just write and don't delete anything. Just keep them keys clicking.

This simple program helps you practice freewriting. It disables arrow-keys, delete and backspace. Yikes. No editing. And to keep you typing it also automatically starts deleting your text if you pause for more than a second.
